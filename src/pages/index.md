What will be the output of the following code?

```java
int n = 10;
while (n-- >= 1) {
  System.out.print(n + " ");
}
```

- `10 9 8 7 6 5 3 2 1`
- `9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2 1`
- `9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2 1 0`
- `10 9 8 7 6 5 3 2 1 0`
- Will not compile because of the syntax error in the condition!
